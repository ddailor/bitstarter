Save Money - "Vampire power" or standby power is electricity that is being used by electrical devices when they are turned off but are still plugged in.

10% of a typical U.S. homes annual electrical bill is due to vampire power. This can add up to more than $400 per year.

Only a few home appliances such as TV's, set-top boxes, stereo's and PC's consume the majority of this "vampire power". Plugging these appliances into a Vamp-Off can reduce your lost power and pay-off quickly.

Save the Environment - 110 million U.S. homes magnify the issue of vampire power.

Five 500MW coal plants could be retired if we reduced 1/3 of the vampire power. This could easily be achieved using the Vamp-Off system.

This would eliminate 7.5 million tons of coal being burned per year.

20 million tons of CO2 would not be added to the atmosphere.

Overall health and longevity of U.S. residents would be improved.

FAQ

What do I need to use the Vamp-Off system?
You need a home network Wi-fi router using 802.11b/g/n and a mobile phone.
How do I find out how much vampire power I am using ?
You need a watt meter that you can plug each appliance into and measure the power being used when off. The "Kill-o-Watt" is a meter available at hardware stores for about $20.
How do I install the Vamp-Off?
Download the mobile app, Plug the Vamp-Off into an outlet, and hold the phone display next to the Vamp-Off and start the set-up procedure.
