Schedule

Review your normal pattern of being home, away, asleep or awake and schedule the "Vamp-Off" to turn off during your asleep, away hours and to turn back on for home, awake hours.

You can program the "Vamp-Off" each day to meet your normal schedule.

The schedule can be over-ridden with manual controls
