The Vamp-Off Products

Here are the products we offer to give you the capability to remotely shut off standby power.

Click the "Learn more" to see how these products work together.

Learn more »

Receiver

The receiver is the unit that you plug the appliance that you want to control into. This will receive the signal from the internet gateway to turn on or off the power to the appliance.

More details »

Gateway

The gateway unit plugs into your home network router using a std CAT-5 cable.

The gateway will communicate with the server in the cloud to send and receive signals from your mobile phone

More details »

Mobile App

From the app you can set-up automatic schedules to turn standby power off or on. The app also allows you to manually toggle the standby power when you are away from home.

The mobile app for Android "Vamp-Off" can be found in the Google Play Store.

The mobile app for iOS "Vamp-Off" can be found in the Apple Store.
