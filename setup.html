Set up the "Vamp-Off"

Download the Vamp-Off mobile app.

Enter the SSID of your home router, the password for your home router and a name for your appliance that you are controlling on the setup page of the app.

Hold the mobile phone display against the clear slit in the "Vamp-Off" case and press the "Sync to Wi-Fi" button.

If there is blinking green light after the procedure - the unit can now communicate with the internet.

If the unit is blinking red or orange, repeat the "Sync to Wi-Fi" procedure until you do see a blinking green light.
