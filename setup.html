Set up the "Vamp-Off"

Download the Vamp-Off mobile app from the appropriate store.
Enter the SSID of your home router and the password for your home router on the setup page of the app.
Enter a nickname for the unit/appliance you are plugging in or room name
Hold the mobile phone display against the clear slit in the "Vamp-Off" case and press the "Blink-Up" button
Make sure you see the unit blinking green from the clear slit after the procedure - the unit can now communicate with the internet
