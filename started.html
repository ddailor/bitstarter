Getting Started

Using the "Vamp-Off" controller is easy.

1. Buy a Vamp-Off Controller and plug into a home outlet.
2. Download the Vamp-Off app to your computer or mobile phone.
3. Use the app to sync the Vamp-Off to your Wi-Fi, plug in a high standby power appliance.
4. Set up your schedule to control automatically or use the App to control manually.

Set up

Enter your wi-fi router SSID and password and a nickname for the Vamp-Off unit. Then start the sync procedure.

Control

Use the mobile phone app to manually turn the appliance standby power on or off.

Schedule

Use the mobile phone app to set a calendar that will automatically turn appliance standby power off or on.
