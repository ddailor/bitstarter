Controller

The controller is a wi-fi enabled transceiver that will use your home wi-fi router to communicate with the scheduler in the internet cloud and your mobile phone app to shut off the standby power to any appliance you have plugged into it.

More details »

Mobile App

From the mobile phone app you can set-up automatic schedules to turn standby power off or on. The mobile phone app also allows you to manually toggle the standby power when you are away from home.

The mobile app "Vamp-Off" is free in the Google Play Store or the Apple Store.
